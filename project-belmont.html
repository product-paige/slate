// The Challenge

Delivering a full cosmetic renovation of 30 occupied units while maintaining tenant comfort and minimising disruption.

Slate Craftsmen was engaged to modernise the 30-unit residential building at 164 Belmont Ave in Kitchener. The project required careful scheduling to ensure each unit was renovated efficiently while residents remained in adjacent units. Our team coordinated all sub-trades, permit issuance, and municipal approvals. Strong relationships with local suppliers allowed us to source materials quickly and keep the project on budget. Cosmetic improvements across kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, and common areas brought the building to a modern standard that significantly increased its rental value.
